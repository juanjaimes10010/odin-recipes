Tinga

A tasty mexican dish. Chicken with chipotle sauce.

Ingredients

  • Garlic and onion
  • Chipotles in adobo sauce
  • canned tomatoes
  • chicken stock
  • Honey (optional)
  • seasonings
  • chicken
  • cilantro
  • cotija cheese
  • avocado
  • sour cream
  • lime

Steps

  1. Make the sauce. Saute the onions and garlic in a bit of oil, then blend them together with the remaing sauce ingredients.
  2. Toss with the chicken. Toss the sauce witht the chicken and a bay leaf and cook for about 5 minutes or so, to help all of those delicious flavors blend together.
  3. Season. As always, don't forget tot taste and season the chicken to see how much salt and pepper it needs.(Also, go ahead and discard that bay leaf!)
  4. Serve. Then serve the chicken warm however you'd like, garnished with lots of fresh toppings.
